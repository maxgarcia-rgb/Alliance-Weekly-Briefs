📐 Metric Definitions & Calculation Methods
Integration GAS vs Overall GAS
Integration GAS = revenue earned specifically through the named integration (Shopify, HubSpot, etc.) — only campaigns that used that integration's data connection. Overall Account GAS = total Gross Ad Spend for that account across ALL StackAdapt campaigns, all channels. Integration GAS is always ≤ Overall GAS for the same account.
Deduplication
If a campaign runs on 2 or more integrations simultaneously, its GAS is divided evenly across those integrations. This prevents the same campaign GAS from being counted in full in multiple integration totals.
Q2 QTD Integration GAS
Actual integration GAS recorded April 1–14, 2026 (14 of 91 days = ~15% of the quarter). Deduped.
Pacing (Linear Run Rate)
QTD integration GAS ÷ 14 days × 91 days. Straight-line projection at current daily rate. Intentionally conservative — no seasonal adjustment.
Model Call — Integration GAS
Estimate of full-quarter integration GAS from three components: (1) Active accounts: QTD daily rate × remaining days, using account-level Q2/Q1 seasonality for HubSpot/Snowflake (2 years of data), linear for Shopify/Klaviyo (ramp year distortion). Blended 60/40 with rep's integration GAS estimate where available and within 3x of model. (2) Watch list: Q1 integration GAS × seasonality × activation probability. (3) Coverage gap: trailing 2Q avg integration GAS × consistency score.
Integration Ratio
Trailing 2-quarter average of (account integration GAS ÷ account overall GAS). Used to convert a rep's overall account forecast into an estimated integration GAS contribution. Based on Q4 2025 + Q1 2026 actuals per account per integration.
Rep Q2 Overall Acct FC
The rep's own forecast for total account GAS in Q2 — covering ALL spend on StackAdapt, not just integration-linked campaigns. This is the number pulled from the SFDC weekly forecast pull.
Integration GAS Est. from Rep FC
Rep Q2 Overall Acct FC × account's trailing integration ratio. Always ≤ the overall rep forecast. Represents the model's estimate of how much of the rep's forecasted spend will flow through the integration specifically.
New Business QTD — Integration GAS
Integration GAS from accounts that had $0 integration activity in Q1 2026. Quarterly cohort definition: active = any integration GAS > $0 in that quarter.
Not Yet Active Q2 — Integration GAS Watch
Sum of Q1 2026 integration GAS from accounts with $0 Q2 activity so far. At day 14 of 91 this is a watch list, NOT confirmed churn — most accounts haven't launched Q2 campaigns yet.
Watch List Probability
Model-assigned likelihood of Q2 activation. Formula: base = consistency score (# active quarters ÷ # available quarters) +15% if active in Q2 2025, +10% if rep has a Q2 forecast, -20% if declining trajectory, +10% if growing. Capped at 95%.
Account Trajectory
Based on last 3 active quarters of integration GAS: Growing = last > first by >10%. Declining = last < first by >10%. Stable = within ±10%. New/Limited = <3 quarters of history.
Forecast Flag ⬇ Risk
Rep's Q2 overall account forecast < 80% of trailing 2Q avg overall account GAS. Filtered to accounts with ≥$10K trailing avg integration GAS. The 'Integration GAS at Stake' column shows how much integration revenue is exposed.
Forecast Flag ⬆ Opportunity
Rep's Q2 overall account forecast > 120% of trailing 2Q avg overall GAS — potential integration GAS upside.
